Diploma in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering

Diploma in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering(DETE) is a diploma level of electronics and telecommunication which is one of the popular courses among engineering aspirants which focuses on the basic elements of electronics.The focus in this discipline is on understanding electronics network and devices,electric magnetic fields,computer fundamentals,communication and control system.It uses the scientific knowledge of the behaviour and effects of electronics to develop conponents,device,systems and equipments.It deals with recent trends in communication technology via wire/wireless networks,circuits design and microcontrollers.
