Final Semester Students Projects

Final year projects

The final year project is the culmination of the degree – it gives students a chance to demonstrate all they have learned. The project module is very different from other modules. Although students are supervised, the onus is on the student to define the problem boundaries, to investigate possible solutions, and to present the results in writing, verbally and in action. Apart from an initial briefing session there are no formal lectures to attend. Teaching consists of regular individual/small group meetings to discuss progress. For assessment, students submit reports of their progress and final results, and give in-person presentations and demonstrations of their work.

    DETE 2015 batch

    1.Electronic ScoreBoard

    In this project “DIGITAL SCOREBOARD”. We used this scoreboard to display the result or the point of the game and other sport items like volleyball, Basketball, Badminton, Table Tennis, Hockey and Rugby etc. In digital score board we can count up the number from 0 to 99. The goal was a product that is easy to use, reliable and efficient for the previously stated purposed.








    Component Used

    1.IC CD45193BE (Binary Counter (1-15).

    2.IC CD4543b (Seven segment Decoder).

    3.IC SN74ls08 AND Gate.

    4.IC NE555N.

    5.Resistor 1K, 10K, 3.3K.

    6.Push Up Non-Lock Switch.

    7.Jumper wire.

    8.Pcb Board.




    Detail

    1.IC CD45193B

    Up/down difference counting.

    Multistage ripple counting

    Synchronous frequency divider.

    A/D and D/A conversion.

    Programmable binary or BCD counting.


    2.IC CD4543B

    Instrument display driver.

    Dashboard display driver.

    Computer/calculator display driver.

    Timing device driver(clocks, watch, timers).


    3.SN74LS08

    4.IC NE555N

    Pulse generation.

    Sequential timing.

    Time delay generation.
